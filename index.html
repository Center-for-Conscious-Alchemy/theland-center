A Place for Shared Healing

Where people come together to reconnect

The Land cultivates connection and whole-person well-being by bringing people together for shared healing experiences rooted in community, reflection, and care for the land.

Our Purpose

Mending the relational fabric of community

Rising disconnection, burnout, and isolation are eroding individual well-being and weakening the social fabric, particularly among those who serve others. Clinical care alone cannot address this loss of belonging, meaning, and connection to place.

The Land exists to create spaces where people can reconnect with themselves, one another, and place.

Founded by mental health practitioners who understand that healing happens in community, The Land brings people together for reflective, place-based experiences that restore connection to self, others, and purpose.

What We Believe

Three truths guide our work

01

Healing is shared

Restoration happens in relationship with people, community, and place, not in isolation. We heal together.

02

Connection is infrastructure

Belonging and relational well-being are foundational to healthy families, workplaces, and civic life.

03

Caregivers deserve renewal

Those who serve others deserve spaces for their own restoration. Supporting them strengthens entire communities.

The Place

135 acres in the Wisconsin woods

Set along the Balsam Branch River, one hour northeast of the Twin Cities. A place for disconnection and connection. For inner and outer exploration.

Lake & River Access

Private shoreline along Ox Lake and the meandering Balsam Branch River for reflection and renewal.

Year-Round Programming

Experiences designed with the seasons and natural rhythms of this land, from summer gatherings to winter retreats.

Gathering & Sleeping Spaces

A commons lodge, private cabins, and platform tents designed for comfort and community.

60 Minutes from MSP

Close enough to access, far enough to truly step away. Easy reach from the Twin Cities and airport.

Experiences

Place-based gatherings for renewal

Our programming fosters reflection, belonging, and integration into daily life. Each experience is designed to strengthen your capacity to show up for yourself and others.

Reflective Retreats

Multi-day experiences combining solitude, guided practice, and communal meals for deep restoration.

Community Gatherings

Seasonal events that bring people together around shared purpose, story, and connection to place.

Therapeutic Programs

Integrative wellness experiences including KAP, breathwork, and mind-body modalities in a supportive setting.

Who We Serve

For those who hold communities together

We prioritize those who serve others, people who often lack spaces for their own restoration. By supporting caregivers and public servants, The Land strengthens the relational infrastructure that allows communities to heal and flourish.

  • Educators and healthcare workers
  • Social workers and nonprofit professionals
  • Veterans and first responders
  • Spiritual leaders and community organizers
